Meet the Wigl Kit!

A DIY robot with a musical ear

The Wigl Kit is a DIY robot package that combines music and electronics to build your very own robot!

Wigl is ALWAYS listening for musical notes, and through some complex math, can hear individual music notes.

What's the "Wigl Kit"?

100% open source software and hardware

Ok, I think I get it.
But... what's a "Wigl"?

Wigl is an interactive robot with a musical ear.

It hears notes (from any instrument) and responds with real-world movements, lights, and special dances!

Through sequenced musical notes, you can even program unique moves! The options are endless.
